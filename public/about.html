Who We Are

Born from a Passion
for Great Food

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Johannesburg, Ember & Oak Grill was born from a simple belief — that great food brings people together. What started as a small burger joint in Sandton quickly grew into one of the city's most loved family dining destinations.

Our name says it all. The Ember represents the real flame we use to grill every burger and cut of meat. The Oak represents the warmth, strength and rootedness we bring to every dining experience.

Today, with two thriving branches and a loyal community of regulars, we remain committed to the same principles we started with — honest food, generous portions, and a space where everyone feels at home.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

To serve honest, flavourful food in a warm and welcoming space where every guest — from the youngest to the oldest — feels completely at home.

Quality First

We never compromise on ingredients. Every burger, every side, every sauce is made with care and the best produce we can source.

Family at Heart

We are built for families. From high chairs to kids menus, we make sure every member of the family has a great time.

Community Driven

We source locally, employ locally and give back to the communities we operate in. Johannesburg built us, and we are proud of it.

Inclusive Menu

We believe great food should be for everyone. Our menu caters to vegetarians, gluten-free diners and families with different needs.
